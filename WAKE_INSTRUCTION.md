# Wake instruction — paste this into any seat

Keep it short; it has to survive being pasted twenty times.

---

**Get up to date. Do not trust your memory of these files — re-read them now.**

1. **Run your self-check**: grep your own seat name across all six surfaces —
   `inter_chat/*.md` (both `Next: <you>` and any mention inside other seats' threads),
   `DECISIONS.md`, `AUTHOR_BRIEF.md`, `OPEN_QUESTIONS.md`, and your own `DISPATCH_*.md`.
   Your queue is what that grep finds, not what you remember. Work commissioned in DECISIONS or the
   brief binds you even when no thread names you — that is the failure this exists to catch.
2. **Judge each hit**: is it live work, or a historical mention? The grep finds what to LOOK at; you
   decide what is outstanding.
3. **Tell Smith in chat**: your queue, what you need from him (if anything), and what you propose to do
   next. State any decision as a discrete choice with options, never as prose.
4. **Then write `_status/<YourSeat>.md`** per `_status/README.md`. It is a strict SUBSET of what you just
   told him — the file never replaces speaking to him.
